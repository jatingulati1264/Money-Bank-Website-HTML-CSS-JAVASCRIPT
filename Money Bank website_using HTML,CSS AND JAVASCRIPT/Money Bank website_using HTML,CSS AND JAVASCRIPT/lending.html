MONEY BANK PROVIDES LOANS AGAINST PROPERTY
Get Loan Against Properties that are best matched to your credit profile
The Money Bank offers two types of Loan Against Property: one is the Money Business Scheme and the second one is Money Mortgage Loan Scheme. In this section, we will take a closer look at the two types or subcategories of the Loan Against Property.

Key features of the Money Bank MORTAGAGE LOAN SCHEME
  • Loan from Money Bank offers overdraft facility against immovable property that can be used for financing personal expenses or to invest in a business, etc.
  • The maximum amount that can be availed through this loan is Rs. 5crores
  • As a form of security, Money bank accepts the mortgage of residential, or commercial or industrial property that should be in the name of the loan applicant along with his or her close relatives, such as the applicants’ spouse, children, parents, or joint owners of the property, or a company or firm.
  • For the Money bank Mortgage Scheme, it is mandatory for the applicant to provide a co-applicant or guarantor.

Loan against the eligibility Criteria
  • Individuals/joint borrowers who are salaried employees or who may be self-employed and professionals may apply for this loan, provided they have proof of previous two years Income Tax Returns
  • Individuals who are engaged in agriculture and its allied activities may apply for this loan
  • Any company or firm may apply for this loan jointly with their directors or partners, provided the security is owned by the director or partner or by the company or firm itself. The income that is generated by the company will also be considered by the bank before approving or rejecting the application

Documents required for the Money Bank Mortgage Loan Scheme
  • Passport size photographs of the loan applicant, co-applicant and guarantor
  • Proof of Residence that may be in the form of a copy of the Ration Card, Telephone Bill, Passport,
  • Electricity Bill or Voters ID Card
  • Proof of Identity that can be in the form of a copy of a Passport, Voter’s ID Card, Driver’s License, PAN Card or Employer’s Card
  • Income Proof in the form of the Income Tax Returns or Form 16 of the past two to three years
  • Copy of all the applicable papers of the property that the applicant is pledging as collateral.
